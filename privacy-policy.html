Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Unblocked Games! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit and use our free online games platform.

2. Information We Collect

Unblocked Games is designed to be used without registration or personal information. We do not require you to create accounts or provide personal details to play our games.

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Browser type and version

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may include games and content from third-party providers. These services may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. We recommend reviewing their policies when interacting with third-party content.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about data we may have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information

  • Object to certain processing activities

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
