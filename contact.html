Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a broken game?

Email us at games@unblockedgames.com with the game name and a description of the issue. We'll investigate and fix it as soon as possible.

Can I suggest new games?

Absolutely! Send your game suggestions to feedback@unblockedgames.com. We're always looking for new and exciting games to add to our collection.

Do you collect personal information?

We prioritize your privacy. No registration is required to play games, and we collect minimal data. Check our Privacy Policy for details.

Why won't a game load?

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the issue persists, please report it to us.
